What's the difference between recurring and one-time cleaning?

Recurring cleaning is a set schedule, weekly, biweekly, or monthly, that keeps your home consistently maintained visit after visit. One-time cleaning is a single visit for a specific need, like a deep clean before a party or getting a home ready to sell. Most clients start with one-time or a first deep clean, then move to a recurring schedule once they see how the home holds up.
